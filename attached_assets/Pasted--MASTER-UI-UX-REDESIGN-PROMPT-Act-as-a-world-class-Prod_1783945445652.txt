# MASTER UI/UX REDESIGN PROMPT

Act as a world-class Product Designer, Creative Director, UX Researcher, Brand Strategist, Motion Designer, and Senior Frontend Engineer with 20+ years of experience designing award-winning luxury websites.

Your task is to completely redesign the website for **Club Exotica**, transforming it from a conventional travel website into a premium luxury travel brand that immediately builds trust, exclusivity, elegance, and aspiration within the first 3 seconds.

This should not resemble a generic WordPress or template-based travel website. It must compete visually with brands like:

* Aman Resorts
* Four Seasons
* Airbnb Luxe
* Ritz-Carlton
* Apple
* Tesla
* Bentley
* Rolls-Royce
* Six Senses
* Marriott Luxury Collection
* One&Only Resorts

## Primary Goal

Create a website that makes users instantly think:

> "This feels premium. I trust this brand. I want to travel with them."

The design should evoke luxury, exclusivity, calmness, sophistication, and emotional desire—not clutter, discounts, or generic tourism.
